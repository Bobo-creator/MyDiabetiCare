Medication Education

Insulin: A hormone that helps regulate blood sugar by allowing glucose to enter cells. It's essential for people with Type 1 and advanced Type 2 diabetes.

Ozempic (Semaglutide): A GLP-1 receptor agonist that helps lower blood sugar by enhancing insulin secretion and reducing appetite. Also helps with weight management.

Metformin: First-line oral medication for Type 2 diabetes. It improves insulin sensitivity and lowers liver glucose production. Most effective when paired with healthy diet and exercise.
